'Collect Some Stuff' is a two player Javascript game where you compete against your friends and the clock, to collect as many points as possible. The player with the most points at the end wins!

This game was created during my time at 'General Assembly'. The brief was to create a two player JS game with Win logic. The project lasted 5 days, resulting in a presentation of our final game.
