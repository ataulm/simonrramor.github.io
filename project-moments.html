'Moments' is an online, image sharing platform that allows users to take photos and privately share them with specific groups.

This product was created during my time at 'General Assembly'. The brief was to create a RESTful Express application with log in. The project lasted 5 days, resulting in a presentation of our application.

'Moments' allows users to register and log in with an email address or Instagram OAuth. Once logged in users can create new groups, selecting the users they wish to include in each group. Users can then post photos to an individual or multiple groups. The photos are then fed into a timeline, and segmented by group and date posted, these are called moments. Users can then comment on moments with friends.

Going forward I plan to add a Google push notification API to alert groups when users have posted images. Users will also be able to set time parameters on notifications, periodically alerting groups to post images of what they are currently at that specific time.

The implementation of push notifications will add an element of social encouragement to increase engagement with the product. Users will post photos into their group's timeline when their friends do, making people feel connected in that one moment, where ever they are in the world.
